Pumpkin Spice Lattes!

The arrival of fall means the arrival of many seasonal, cozy flavors! Pumpkin spice, pecans, cinnamon, and more flood pantries and stores as leaves turn orange and red. Today, I'll be teaching you all a simple pumpkin spice latte recipe to welcome in the fall!

Ingredients

A traditional iced Latte doesn't take many ingredients, but a PSL does! You'll need:

  • 1 tbsp Pumkin Pie Spice
  • 1/2 cup Pumpkin Puree
  • 2 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 cup Water
  • 1/2 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Maple Syrup
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Coffee to taste(Espresso or instant works! I'll be using the instant coffee method.)
  • Milk to taste(any milk works)

Recipe

The Syrup!

  1. First we have to make the pumpkin spice syrup! Add in Water, brown sugar, pumpkin pie spice, pumpkin puree, maple syrup, and salt in a pan and simmer it for 5 mins. Stir occasionally.
  2. Take pan off the stove and add in vanilla extract. Stir!
  • Side note: If you want a more detailed recipe of this syrup, check out this website!
The Latte!
  1. Make the espresso(For instant coffee, put a tablesppon of coffee powder and add hot water just enough for the powder to dissolve)
  2. Add in syrup to the espresso to taste and mix!
  3. In a seperate cup, add ice and milk. Add in espresso and stir. Optional: Add pumpkin spice cold foam or whipped cream!
  4. Enjoy!
