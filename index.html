Fall Flavors!

The arrival of fall means the arrival of many seasonal, cozy flavors! Pumpkin spice, pecans, cinnamon, and more flood pantries and stores as leaves turn orange and red. Today, I will be teaching you all a simple pumpkin spice latte recipe to welcome in the fall!

Ingredients

A traditional iced Latte doesn't take many ingredients, but a PSL does! You'll need: - 1 tbsp Pumkin Pie Spice - 1/2 cup Pumpkin Puree - 2 tsp Vanilla extract - 1 cup Water - 1/2 cup Brown Sugar - 1/4 cup Maple Syrup - Pinch of Salt - Coffee to taste(Espresso or instant works! I'll be using the instant coffee method.) - Milk to taste(any milk works)
